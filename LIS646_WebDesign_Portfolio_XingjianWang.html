A Good Story For Future

As we work to practice and expand our understand of design thinking, human-centered and life-centered design this semester, we will need to understand long-term thinking (“longtermism”) and what makes design distinct from other forms of thinking from a theoretical perspective, a lived experience perspective and a self-reflective perspective.

First Nation Landscape Tour Experience Map

Maps help us navigate the world, but have also been a means to knowledge and ownership, especially in a settler colonial context such as the United States. Surveying the land and then mapping it by Western conventions we may overlook as commonplace are practices that facilitated the seizure of land and the displacement of First Peoples.
